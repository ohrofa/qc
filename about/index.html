    QuranO.com - Qur'an Online. Qurano is Quran Online in 7 languages (Currently) with Comprehensive translation and Tafsir. Word by word Quran translation is avalaible in Bangla/Bengali, Deutsch, English, Indonesian (Perkata), Turkish, and Urdu. There are Quran audio reciter and various tafsir as well. We considerably adding another languages for future updates. We focus on easy navigation, lightweight and full features as references in one place.
