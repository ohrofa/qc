    Diyanet Isleri:

    Sana vahyedilene uy; Allah hükmünü verene kadar sabret. O, hüküm verenlerin en iyisidir.

    English Sahih:

    And follow what is revealed to you, [O Muhammad], and be patient until Allah will judge. And He is the best of judges.
