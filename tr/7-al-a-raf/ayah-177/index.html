    Diyanet Isleri:

    Ayetlerimizi yalan sayan, kendine zulmeden millet ne kötü bir misaldir!

    English Sahih:

    How evil an example [is that of] the people who denied Our signs and used to wrong themselves.

    4 Ali Fikri Yavuz
