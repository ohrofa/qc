    `aynen fîhâ tüsemmâ selsebîlâ. (al-ʾInsān 76:18)

    Diyanet Isleri:

    O pınara "Selsebil" denir.

    English Sahih:

    [From] a fountain within it [i.e., Paradise] named Salsabeel. ([76] Al-Insan : 18)
