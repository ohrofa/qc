    Diyanet Isleri:

    Biz de, onu ve askerlerini yakalayıp suya attık. Zalimlerin sonunun nasıl olduğuna bir bak.

    English Sahih:

    So We took him and his soldiers and threw them into the sea. So see how was the end of the wrongdoers.
