    Wa'ada Allahu almumineena waalmuminati jannatin tajree min tahtiha alanharu khalideena feeha wamasakina tayyibatan fee jannati 'adnin waridwanun mina Allahi akbaru thalika huwa alfawzu al'atheemu

    Sahih International:
