    Wa ammaa man ootiya kitaabahoo bishimaalihee fa yaqoolu yaalaitanee lam oota kitaaabiyah (al-Ḥāq̈q̈ah 69:25)

    Sahih International:

    But as for he who is given his record in his left hand, he will say, "Oh, I wish I had not been given my record (Al-Haqqah [69] : 25)

    1 Mufti Taqi Usmani

    As for him who will be given his book in his left hand, he will say, “ Oh, would that I had not been given my book,

    2 Dr. Mustafa Khattab, the Clear Quran

    And as for those given their record in their left hand, they will cry ˹bitterly˺, “I wish I had not been given my record,

    3 Ruwwad Translation Center

    27 Tafseer Ibn Kathir

    The Bad Condition of Whoever is given His Record in His Left Hand

    These Ayat inform about the condition of the wretched people when one of them is given his Record (of deeds) in his left hand when the people are brought before Allah. At this time he will be very remorseful.
