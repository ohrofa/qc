    Fabi ayyi aalaaa'i Rabbikumaa tukazzibaan. (ar-Raḥmān 55:49)

    Sahih International:

    So which of the favors of your Lord would you deny? (Ar-Rahman [55] : 49)

    1 Mufti Taqi Usmani

    So, which of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?

    2 Dr. Mustafa Khattab, the Clear Quran

    Then which of your Lord’s favours will you both deny?

    3 Ruwwad Translation Center

    Then which of the favors of your Lord will you [men and jinn] deny?

    4 A. J. Arberry

    O which of your Lord's bounties will you and you deny?

    5 Abdul Haleem

    Which, then, of your Lord’s blessings do you both deny?

    6 Abdul Majid Daryabadi

    Which, then, of the benefits of your Lord will ye twain belie?

    7 Abdullah Yusuf Ali

    Then which of the favours of your Lord will ye deny?-

    8 Abul Ala Maududi

    Which of your Lord's favours will you twain – you men and jinn – then deny?

    9 Ahmed Ali

    Which of the favours of your Lord will you then deny? --

    10 Ahmed Raza Khan

    So O men and jinns! Which favour of your Lord will you deny?

    11 Ali Quli Qarai

    So which of your Lord’s bounties will you both deny?

    12 Ali Ünal

    Then, (O humankind and jinn) which of the favors of your Lord will you deny?

    13 Amatul Rahman Omar

    Which of the benefactions of your Lord will you twain, then, deny?
