    Surah An-Nisa' Verse 68

    وَلَهَدَيْنَٰهُمْ
    And We would have guided them
    صِرَٰطًا
    (to the) way.
    مُّسْتَقِيمًا
    (the) straight.

    Walahadaynahum siratan mustaqeeman

    And We would have guided them to a straight path.

    • Tafsir Ibn Kathir
    • Tafsir Al-Jalalayn
    • Tafsir Yusuf Ali

    And indeed We should have guided them to the straight way.

    in this life and the Hereafter.
    Whoever Obeys Allah and His Messenger Will Be Honored by Allah

    Allah then said,

    وَمَن يُطِعِ اللّهَ وَالرَّسُولَ فَأُوْلَـيِكَ مَعَ الَّذِينَ أَنْعَمَ اللّهُ عَلَيْهِم مِّنَ النَّبِيِّينَ وَالصِّدِّيقِينَ وَالشُّهَدَاء وَالصَّالِحِينَ وَحَسُنَ أُولَـيِكَ رَفِيقًا

    And We would have guided them to a straight path. Some Companions said to the Prophet (s); `How will we be able to see you in Paradise, when you will be in the highest stations and we will be lower than you?', and so the following was revealed;

    And We should have shown them the Straight Way.
