    Wa furushim marfoo'ah (al-Wāqiʿah 56:34)

    Sahih International:

    And [upon] beds raised high. (Al-Waqi'ah [56] : 34)

    and mattresses of high quality.

    and upraised couches.

    with incomparable companions

    6 Abdul Majid Daryabadi

    7 Abdullah Yusuf Ali

    And maidens incomparable.

    13 Amatul Rahman Omar

    14 English Literal

    15 Faridul Haque

    And raised couches.

    16 Hamid S. Aziz

    And on couches, raised high.
