    Surah Abasa Verse 38

    وُجُوهٌ
    Faces,
    يَوْمَئِذٍ
    that Day
    مُّسْفِرَةٌ
    (will be) bright,

    Wujoohun yawmaithin musfiratun

    [Some] faces, that Day, will be bright -

    • Tafsir Ibn Kathir
    • Tafsir Al-Jalalayn
    • Tafsir Yusuf Ali

    Some faces that Day will be bright (Musfirah),

    meaning, the people will be divided into two parties. There will be faces that are Musfirah, which means bright.

    ضَاحِكَةٌ مُّسْتَبْشِرَةٌ

    On that day some faces will be shining, radiant,

    Some faces that Day will be beaming,
