    Qaala inna Rasoolakumul lazee ursila ilaikum lamajnoon (aš-Šuʿarāʾ 26:27)

    Sahih International:

    [Pharaoh] said, "Indeed, your 'messenger' who has been sent to you is mad." (Ash-Shu'ara [26] : 27)

    1 Mufti Taqi Usmani

    He (Pharaoh) said, “Your messenger who is sent to you is a mad man indeed.”

    قَالَ

    He (Fir`awn ) said;

    إِنَّ رَسُولَكُمُ الَّذِي أُرْسِلَ إِلَيْكُمْ لَمَجْنُونٌ



    Verily, your Messenger who has been sent to you is a madman!

    meaning, there is no sense in his claim that there is any god other than me!
