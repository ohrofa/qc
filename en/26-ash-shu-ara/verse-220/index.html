    Surah Ash-Shu'ara Verse 220

    إِنَّهُۥ
    Indeed, He
    هُوَ
    [He]
    ٱلسَّمِيعُ
    (is) the All-Hearer,
    ٱلْعَلِيمُ
    the All-Knower.

    Innahu huwa alssamee'u al'aleemu

    Indeed, He is the Hearing, the Knowing.

    • Tafsir Ibn Kathir
    • Tafsir Al-Jalalayn
    • Tafsir Yusuf Ali

    Verily, He, only He, is the All-Hearer, the All-Knower.

    He hears all that His servants say and He knows all their movements, as He says;

    وَمَا تَكُونُ فِى شَأْنٍ وَمَا تَتْلُواْ مِنْهُ مِن قُرْءَانٍ وَلَا تَعْمَلُونَ مِنْ عَمَلٍ إِلاَّ كُنَّا عَلَيْكُمْ شُهُودًا إِذْ تُفِيضُونَ فِيهِ

    Neither you do any deed nor recite any portion of the Qur'an, nor you do any deed, but We are Witness thereof, when you are doing it. (10;61

    Truly He is the Hearing, the Knowing.

    For it is He Who heareth and knoweth all things.
