    Sahih International:

    Arise [to pray] the night, except for a little – (Al-Muzzammil [73] : 2)

    Ibn `Abbas, Ad-Dahhak and As-Suddi all said,
    يَا أَيُّهَا الْمُزَّمِّلُ
    (O you wrapped up!)

    "This means, O you who are asleep."

    Qatadah said,

    "The one who is wrapped up in his garments."
