    Wa Yawma yu'radul lazeena kafaroo 'alan naari alaisa haaza bil haqq; qaaloo balaa wa Rabbinaa; qaala fazooqul 'azaaba bimaa kuntum takfuroon

    Sahih International:

    And the Day those who disbelieved are exposed to the Fire [it will be said], "Is this not the truth?" They will say, "Yes, by our Lord." He will say, "Then taste the punishment for what you used to deny."

    27 Tafseer Ibn Kathir

    وَيَوْمَ يُعْرَضُ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا عَلَى النَّارِ أَلَيْسَ هَذَا بِالْحَقِّ

    And on the Day when those who disbelieve will be exposed to the Fire (it will be said to them);"Is this not the truth?"

    meaning, it will be said to them, "Isn't this the truth? Is this magic?, or do you not see?"

    قَالُوا بَلَى وَرَبِّنَا

    They will say;"Yes, by our Lord!"

    meaning, they will have no choice but to confess.
