    Yad'oona feehaa bikulli faakihatin aamineen (ad-Dukhān 44:55)

    Sahih International:

    They will call therein for every [kind of] fruit – safe and secure. (Ad-Dukhan [44] : 55)

    1 Mufti Taqi Usmani

    They will call therein for every fruit peacefully.

    2 Dr. Mustafa Khattab, the Clear Quran

    There they will call for every fruit in serenity.

    3 Ruwwad Translation Center

    They will call therein for every kind of fruit, safe and secure.

    4 A. J. Arberry

    therein calling for every fruit, secure.

    5 Abdul Haleem

    Secure and contented, they will call for every kind of fruit.

    6 Abdul Majid Daryabadi

    They will call therein for every kind of fruit in security.

    7 Abdullah Yusuf Ali

    There can they call for every kind of fruit in peace and security;

    8 Abul Ala Maududi

    While resting in security, they shall call for all kinds of fruit.

    9 Ahmed Ali

    They will call for every kind of fruit with satisfaction.

    10 Ahmed Raza Khan

    In it they will ask for all kinds of fruit, with safety.

    11 Ali Quli Qarai

    Secure [from any kind of harm,] there they will call for every kind of fruit [they wish].

    12 Ali Ünal

    There they call for every kind of fruit in security (from any harm).

    13 Amatul Rahman Omar

    They will order therein every (kind of) fruit, and will be safe and secure.

    14 English Literal

    They call in it with all fruits safe/secure .

    15 Faridul Haque

    In it they will ask for all kinds of fruit, with safety.

    16 Hamid S. Aziz

    They shall call in safety therein for every fruit;

    17 Hilali & Khan

    They will call therein for every kind of fruit in peace and security;

    18 Maulana Mohammad Ali

    Thus (shall it be). And We shall join them to pure, beautiful ones.

    19 Mohammad Habib Shakir

    They shall call therein for every fruit in security;

    20 Mohammed Marmaduke William Pickthall

    They call therein for every fruit in safety.

    21 Muhammad Sarwar

    They will be offered all kinds of fruits, in peace and security.

    22 Qaribullah & Darwish

    There in security, they will call for every kind of fruit.

    23 Safi-ur-Rahman al-Mubarakpuri

    They will call therein for every kind of fruit in peace and security;

    24 Wahiduddin Khan

    They will call therein for every kind of fruit, in peace and security.

    25 Talal Itani

    They will call therein for every kind of fruit, in peace and security.

    26 Tafsir jalalayn

    They will call, they will request from servants, therein, that is, in Paradise, to bring, every fruit, thereof, [remaining] secure, [in their knowledge] that it will not come to an end or cause harm and [secure] from all danger (minna, `secure', is a circumstantial qualifier').

    27 Tafseer Ibn Kathir

    They will call therein for every kind of fruit in peace and security;

    means, whatever kinds of fruit they ask for will be brought to them, and they will have the security of knowing that this supply will never come to an end or be withheld; these fruits will be brought to them whenever they want
