    سَنُقْرِوُكَ


    We shall make you to recite,

    meaning, `O Muhammad.'

    فَلَ تَنسَى




    so you shall not forget (it),

    This is Allah informing and promising him (the Prophet) that He will teach him a recitation that he will not forget
