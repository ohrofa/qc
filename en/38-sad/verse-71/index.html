    The Story of Adam and Iblis

    Allah Tells,

    إِذْ قَالَ رَبُّكَ لِلْمَلَيِكَةِ إِنِّي خَالِقٌ بَشَرًا مِن طِينٍ



    (Remember) when your Lord said to the angels;"Truly, I am going to create man from clay."

    فَإِذَا سَوَّيْتُهُ وَنَفَخْتُ فِيهِ مِن رُّوحِي فَقَعُوا لَهُ سَاجِدِينَ

    Mention, when your Lord said to the angels, `Indeed I am about to create a human being out of clay, and this was Adam.

    Behold, thy Lord said to the angels; "I am about to create man from clay;
