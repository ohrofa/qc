    Sahih International:

    Indeed, mankind was created anxious:

    1 Mufti Taqi Usmani

    Indeed man is created weak in courage,

    2 Dr. Mustafa Khattab, the Clear Quran

    Indeed, humankind was created impatient:

    3 Ruwwad Translation Center

    Indeed, man was created impatient:

    4 A. J. Arberry

    Surely man was created fretful,

    5 Abdul Haleem

    Man was truly created anxious:

    6 Abdul Majid Daryabadi

    Verily man is formed impatient.

    7 Abdullah Yusuf Ali

    Truly man was created very impatient;-

    8 Abul Ala Maududi

    Verily man is impatient by nature:

    9 Ahmed Ali

    Surely man is greedy by nature.

    10 Ahmed Raza Khan

    Indeed man is created very impatient, greedy.

    11 Ali Quli Qarai

    Indeed man has been created covetous:

    12 Ali Ünal

    Surely human has been created with a restless, impatient disposition.

    13 Amatul Rahman Omar

    Verily, such a person is impatient and miserly by nature.

    14 English Literal

    That truly the human/mankind was created worrisome/frightened .

    15 Faridul Haque

    Indeed man is created very impatient, greedy.

    16 Hamid S. Aziz

    Surely man is created anxious (or impatient)

    17 Hilali & Khan

    Verily, man (disbeliever) was created very impatient;

    18 Maulana Mohammad Ali

    And niggardly when good befalls him --

    19 Mohammad Habib Shakir

    Surely man is created of a hasty temperament

    20 Mohammed Marmaduke William Pickthall

    Lo! man was created anxious,

    21 Muhammad Sarwar

    Human beings are created greedy.

    22 Qaribullah & Darwish

    Indeed, the human was created grudging and impatient.

    23 Safi-ur-Rahman al-Mubarakpuri

    Verily, man was created very impatient;

    24 Wahiduddin Khan

    Indeed, man is born impatient:

    25 Talal Itani

    Man was created restless.

    26 Tafsir jalalayn

    Indeed man was created restless; (hal`an is an implied circumstantial qualifier, the explanation of which [follows]);

    27 Tafseer Ibn Kathir

    Man is Impatient. Allah informs about man and his inclination to corrupt his behavior.

    Allah says,

    إِنَّ الاِْنسَانَ خُلِقَ هَلُوعًا




    Verily, man was created very impatient;

    Then, Allah explains this statement by saying,

    إِذَا مَسَّهُ الشَّرُّ جَزُوعًا
