    Sahih International:

    Indeed, We thus reward the doers of good.

    1 Mufti Taqi Usmani

    This is how We reward those who are good in their deeds.

    2 Dr. Mustafa Khattab, the Clear Quran

    Indeed, this is how We reward the good-doers.

    3 Ruwwad Translation Center

    This is how We reward those who do good.

    4 A. J. Arberry

    Even so We recompense the good-doers;

    5 Abdul Haleem

    This is how We reward those who do good:

    6 Abdul Majid Daryabadi

    Verily We! thus We recompense the well-doers.

    7 Abdullah Yusuf Ali

    Thus indeed do We reward those who do right.

    8 Abul Ala Maududi

    Thus do We reward the good-doers.

    9 Ahmed Ali

    That is how We reward those who do good.

    10 Ahmed Raza Khan

    This is how We reward the virtuous.

    11 Ali Quli Qarai

    Thus indeed do We reward the virtuous.

    12 Ali Ünal

    Thus do We reward those devoted to doing good as if seeing God.

    13 Amatul Rahman Omar

    That is how do We reward the performers of excellent deeds.

    14 English Literal

    We (E), like that We reimburse the good doers.

    15 Faridul Haque

    This is how We reward the virtuous.

    16 Hamid S. Aziz

    Even thus do We reward the doers of good.

    Verily, thus do We reward the Muhsinun (good-doers - see V. 2:112).

    19 Mohammad Habib Shakir

    Verily, thus do We reward the doers of good.

    24 Wahiduddin Khan

    This is how We reward those who do good:

    25 Talal Itani

    Thus We reward the righteous.

    26 Tafsir jalalayn

    So, just as We rewarded them both, do We reward the virtuous.
