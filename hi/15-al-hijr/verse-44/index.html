    Muhammad Faruq Khan Sultanpuri & Muhammad Ahmed:

    उसके सात द्वार है। प्रत्येक द्वार के लिए एक ख़ास हिस्सा होगा।'

    English Sahih:

    It has seven gates; for every gate is of them [i.e., Satan's followers] a portion designated."
